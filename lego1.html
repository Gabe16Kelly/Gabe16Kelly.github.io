LEGO SAW SCENE

BLENDER - MODELLING - TEXTURING - RIGGING - LEGO


After watching Spiderman Into the Spiderverse, I was very intrigued by the lego Dimension and after reading the story behind it I was very motivated to create my own lego models. I decided to start of something small with the first Saw movie. I first created the model for my scene in MecaBricks, this allowed me to import them in blender with the MecaBrick addon. From their I rigged the lego models so I could position them correctly, finally I added the lighting, which I believe completes the scene giving off a eery feeling with fanatasic refelctions of the walls and ceiling.
