AT AT

MAYA - MODELLING - TEXTURING - RIGGING


I was tasked with creating 5 game assets in Maya to be used together in a demo game, for a week I was bouncing back and fourth between ideas from Hobbits to Robots to Mutant Man Eating Plants, then I just said screw it I love Star Wars I'm going to make a AT AT.

You can read my report on this project here

DEVELOPMENT


INSPIRATION

Throughout this project I was definitely inspired by the low poly modelling style, I found it quite compatible with Unitys software due to the need for few faces while being able to give of a good amount of detail. I also felt comfortable and in control while hard surface modelling due to the simple structure making it feel like I was putting together a Lego set.
